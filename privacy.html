Privacy Policy

  1. Introduction
    1. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information collected through our restaurant website. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors, and we will use personal information only in ways that are compatible with this Privacy Policy.
  2. Information Collection and Use
    1. We collect personal information from website visitors when they voluntarily submit information to us, such as when they make a reservation or sign up for our newsletter. The information we collect may include:
      • Name
      • Email address
      • Phone number
      • Reservation details (date, time, party size)
      • Dietary preferences or restrictions
      • Payment information (credit card details)
    2. We use this information to:
      • Confirm reservations and communicate with customers about their reservations
      • Send marketing communications, such as newsletters and special offers, to customers who have opted-in to receive them
      • Process payments for reservations
  3. Disclosure of Information
    1. We may share personal information with third parties, such as:
      • Service providers who help us operate our business, such as website hosting providers, payment processors, and email marketing platforms
      • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect our legal rights
      • Business partners, if we have a co-marketing agreement or joint event
    2. We require our service providers to use personal information only for the purpose for which it was provided, and to protect it in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws and regulations.
  4. Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies
    1. We use cookies and other tracking technologies to improve the functionality and performance of our website, and to personalize the user experience. Cookies are small files that are stored on a user's device when they visit a website. They enable us to recognize a user's device and remember their preferences or actions.
    2. We use cookies to:
      • Analyze website usage and performance
      • Personalize content and recommendations
      • Remember user preferences, such as language and location
      • Provide targeted advertising
    3. Most web browsers allow users to control cookies through their browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
    4. Data Retention and Security
      1. We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. We have implemented security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and use. However, no security system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of personal information.
    5. Access and Correction
      1. Website visitors have the right to access and correct their personal information. If you would like to access or correct your personal information, please contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this Privacy Policy.
    6. Changes to this Privacy Policy
      1. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices or legal requirements. We will post a notice on our website if we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy.
